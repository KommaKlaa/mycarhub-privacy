Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-18

1. Who we are

This privacy policy applies to the MyCarHub mobile app ("MyCarHub", "the app"). If you have questions, contact us at dev@mycarhub.app.

2. Data we process

We currently use Supabase Authentication (Google sign-in) for account login. This typically involves processing your email address and a user identifier to provide the login feature.

The app may process photos, videos, and text that you choose to provide (e.g., for OCR scanning of receipts/documents). This processing is performed on your device (offline) unless you explicitly share/export content.

3. Location

If you use the "nearby fuel stations" feature, the app uses your device location to request nearby station prices from the fuel price API. Location is used only to provide this feature.

4. Sharing and uploads

The app does not automatically upload your photos/videos/documents to us. If you choose to share or export content (e.g., to WhatsApp or a cloud service), this is initiated by you.

5. Data retention

The app stores vehicles and related app data locally on your device (offline). Authentication data is retained as long as your account exists.

6. Account deletion

You can delete your account directly in the MyCarHub app via Profile → Delete account. Deleting your account removes your authentication account (Supabase Auth / Google sign-in).

Vehicles and related app data are stored locally on your device (offline). Account deletion does not automatically remove local data. To remove local data, uninstall the app or clear the app storage in your device settings.

For more details, see our Account Deletion page.

7. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your data. To exercise your rights, contact us at dev@mycarhub.app.
