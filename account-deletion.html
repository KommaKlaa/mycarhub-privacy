Account Deletion – MyCarHub

MyCarHub currently uses Supabase Authentication (Google sign-in) for accounts. We do not store your vehicles or other app content on our servers at this time.

How to delete your account

To request deletion of your MyCarHub account (authentication record), please email us:
Email: dev@mycarhub.app

What to include

  • The Google email address you used to sign in

What we delete

  • Your MyCarHub account (Supabase authentication user record)

Local (offline) data on your device

Vehicles and related app data are stored locally on your device (offline). Account deletion does not automatically remove local data. To delete local data, uninstall the app or clear the app storage in your device settings.

Processing time

We process deletion requests within 30 days.
