Account Deletion – MyCarHub

To request deletion of your MyCarHub account and associated data, please email us:

Email: dev@mycarhub.app

What to include

  • The email address you used to sign in (Google account email)
  • Optional: any details that help us identify your account (e.g., screenshots)

What we delete

  • Your account
  • Your stored vehicles and related app data

Processing time

We process deletion requests within 30 days. Backup data, if any, may be retained for a limited time for security/legal reasons.
