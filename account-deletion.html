Account Deletion

This page explains how to delete your MyCarHub account and what happens to your data.

Delete your account in the app

You can delete your account directly inside the MyCarHub app:

1) Open MyCarHub
2) Go to Profile
3) Tap Delete account and confirm

Important: Account deletion removes your authentication account (Supabase Auth / Google sign-in). It does not automatically remove offline data stored on your device.

What we delete

When you delete your account in the app, we delete: • Your MyCarHub account (Supabase authentication user record).

Local (offline) data on your device

MyCarHub stores vehicles and related app data locally on your device (offline). Deleting your account does not automatically remove local data. To delete local data, uninstall the app or clear the app storage in your device settings.

Need help?

If you have issues deleting your account in the app, contact us:

dev@mycarhub.app

Processing time

Account deletion is initiated from within the app. If manual assistance is needed, requests are handled within 30 days.
